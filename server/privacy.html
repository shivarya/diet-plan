Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Diet Plan ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

Diet Plan is a high-protein, low-carb weekly meal planner designed for personalized nutrition guidance.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect and store:

  • Email address — for account authentication and identification
  • Google ID token — securely verified and not stored permanently
  • Account creation date — for user analytics

2. Dietary Preferences & Rules

To provide personalized meal planning, we collect and store your dietary choices:

  • Diet type: Vegetarian, egg-inclusive, or non-vegetarian
  • Daily restrictions: Onion/garlic avoidance (e.g., no onion/garlic on Thursdays)
  • Optional meal inclusions: Brunch, evening snack preferences
  • Kid add-on: Whether there's a child at home requiring kid-friendly dishes
  • Accompaniment preferences: Bread/rice side-dish inclusion
  • Nutritional targets: Protein, calcium, and calorie goals

3. Premium Account Data

If you use premium features (AI-generated meal plans, "Cook from Ingredients"), we track:

  • Premium status: Whether your account has access to paid features
  • Billing information: Payment method and transaction history (processed by Google Play)

4. Generated Meal Plans

We store your generated meal plans, including:

  • Weekly meal schedules
  • Recipe selections and shuffles
  • Timestamps of plan generation
  • Plan history (current + past week's plans)

5. AI Feature Interactions

For premium AI features, we process:

  • AI plan requests: Dietary rules sent to generate personalized plans
  • Ingredient queries: Ingredients you input for "Cook from Ingredients" feature
  • AI interactions: Logged for quality improvement (anonymized)

6. Device & App Metrics

We collect non-personal technical data:

  • App version and build number
  • Device type and OS version
  • App crash reports and error logs
  • Feature usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  1. Personalized Meal Planning: Your dietary rules generate customized weekly meal schedules
  2. Account Management: Email verification, password recovery, account security
  3. Premium Features: AI meal generation, ingredient-based recipe recommendations
  4. App Improvement: Usage analytics to fix bugs and add features
  5. Customer Support: Contact information for assistance requests

What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ We do NOT sell your dietary or health data
  • ❌ We do NOT share meal plans with third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for advertising or marketing (except our own promotional emails)
  • ❌ We do NOT track you across other apps or websites
  • ❌ We do NOT profile you for discrimination

Data Storage & Security

Server Infrastructure

  • Hosting: cPanel shared hosting at shivarya.dev
  • Database: MySQL 8.0+ with encrypted connections
  • Backups: Regular automated backups for disaster recovery

Data Protection

  • Transport Security: All data transmitted via HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Storage: Passwords hashed using bcrypt; sensitive fields encrypted at rest
  • Access Control: Role-based access; admin accounts require strong authentication
  • Session Management: JWT tokens (JSON Web Tokens) for secure API authentication

Data Retention

  • Your account and meal plans are retained as long as your account is active
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days
  • Backup copies may take up to 90 days to be purged
  • We retain anonymized usage logs for up to 1 year for analytics

Data Sharing & Third Parties

AI Provider (Groq)

For premium AI meal planning:

  • Your dietary rules only are sent to Groq API for AI processing
  • We do NOT send your email or personal identifiers
  • Groq processes queries under their own privacy policy: https://groq.com/privacy/
  • Requests are minimally logged for debugging; Groq does not train on your data

Google Sign-In

We verify your identity through Google's OAuth 2.0 service:

  • Google provides us an ID token; we verify and discard it
  • Your Google account data stays with Google; we only store the email you authorize
  • See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No Other Third Parties

We do NOT share your data with:

  • ❌ Advertising networks
  • ❌ Social media platforms
  • ❌ Health or fitness apps
  • ❌ Data brokers
  • ❌ Any other commercial entities

Your Rights & Control

Access Your Data

  • View your meal plans, preferences, and account details in the app
  • Request a data export in JSON format by emailing us

Modify Your Data

  • Edit your dietary rules and preferences anytime via Settings
  • Update or change your meal plan generation parameters
  • Change or re-authorize your email via account settings

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual meal plans
  • Delete your entire account (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Account deletion is permanent; all data is removed within 30 days

Permissions

Required Permissions (Android)

  • INTERNET — API communication with our servers
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Check connectivity before syncing

Optional Permissions

  • CAMERA — Photo upload or scanning (if implemented for custom recipes)
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — Export meal plans to device storage

All permissions are requested transparently. You can revoke them anytime via Android Settings.

Dietary management by parents on behalf of a child is at the parent's discretion and responsibility. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.
